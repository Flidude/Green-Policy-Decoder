About Us

About Green Policy Decoder

Our Mission

At Green Policy Decoder, our mission is to bridge the gap between complex climate policies and the general public. We believe that understanding environmental practices is crucial for making informed decisions and fostering a more sustainable future. Through comprehensive analysis and user-friendly content, we strive to make green policies accessible to everyone.


Who We Are

Green Policy Decoder was founded as a project for the BT Young Scientist competition by Aidan Keane and Niall Nestor. Committed to making a positive impact on climate education, we are dedicated to delivering reliable information about companies' climate policies and environmentally friendly practices.


What Sets Us Apart

  • Transparency: We believe in transparency and aim to provide accurate and up-to-date information about environmental policies.
  • Accessibility: Our content is designed to be accessible to a wide audience, regardless of their background or expertise in environmental issues.
  • Education: We view our platform as an educational resource, empowering individuals to contribute to a more sustainable world.
