What is Green Policy Decoder?

Green Policy Decoder is a website that we made to help people make better informed decisions about the companies they buy from. We are not affiliated to any of the companies mentioned in this website and all our summaries are based on the information provided by the companies themselves.

We made this website for the BT Young Scientist and Technology Exhibition 2024.

Other Climate Resources

  • The Climate Jargon Buster

    • The Climate Jargon Buster is a website run by the Department of the Taoiseach. This website provides definitions for common climate terms. It gives simple to understand definitions for each term. Type the climate related term you want to find the definition for into the search bar and click search. The website will then display the definition for that term.


  • United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change

    • This website is run by the United Nations to keep people up to date with current climate change news and global movements that are combating climate change.
