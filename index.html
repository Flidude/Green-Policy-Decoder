What is Green Policy Decoder?

Green Policy Decoder is a website that aims to make it easier for you to understand the climate policies of large companies and organisations. We have summarised the climate policies of some of the largest companies in Ireland and provided links to their full climate policies. We have also provided a glossary of climate terms and a list of other climate resources.
